Professional Overview

Research-focused GIS professional with expertise in soil yield modeling and sedimentation analysis. Contributes to early warning systems for agriculture using satellite data and spatial intelligence

Advance Research and development; aim at contributing in research and development in GIS and remote sensing, exploring new methodologies in spatial data analysis, image processing and geospatial modeling

Elizabeth is a Kenyan GIS and Remote Sensing specialist with strong expertise in spatial data analysis, geospatial modeling, and environmental monitoring. She is proficient in GIS software (ArcGIS, QGIS, ERDAS, ENVI, Google Earth Engine), programming (Python, JavaScript, CSS, Flask), and database management (SQL), as well as skilled in using field survey tools such as RTK, DRTK, and Total Station. Her experience includes crop data collection, spatial analysis, project development, GIS database creation, system integration, cartographic mapping, and remote sensing analysis. She has worked on projects such as developing an early warning system for maize yield monitoring using machine learning and modeling sediment loads in river basins. She has also presented at professional conferences and is passionate about consultation, mentorship , teamwork, leadership, and community development. Interest: Hiking,
